Hugging Face

Useful Models

Hugging Face is a collaborative platform where the machine learning community comes together to work on models, datasets, and applications. Here are some key points about Hugging Face:

1

Models: Hugging Face hosts over 615,049 models across various domains. These models include state-of-the-art machine learning models for tasks like text generation, visual question answering, and more1.

1

Datasets: The platform offers access to 100,000+ datasets. These datasets cover a wide range of topics and can be used for training and evaluation1.

1

Applications (Spaces): Hugging Face provides 150,000+ demo apps (Spaces) that showcase the capabilities of different models. These applications span text, image, video, audio, and even 3D modalities

1

Community: Over 50,000 organizations use Hugging Face, including companies like Amazon Web Services, Google, and Microsoft. The platform fosters collaboration and knowledge sharing among ML practitioners

Prompt Base

Useful Prompt Templates

PromptBase is a unique marketplace designed for buying and selling high-quality prompts used in programming AI models like DALL·E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and GPT. It addresses the challenge of finding quality prompts online and provides a platform for those skilled in prompt engineering to monetize their abilities1. The platform offers a Prompt Marketplace where you can explore over 100,000 AI prompts from some of the world’s best AI creators. These prompts cover a wide range of creative and practical applications, from logo design to surreal art, and more. PromptBase also allows creators to build their own tiny prompt stores and sell their prompts to the community. Whether you’re looking for inspiration or want to share your own creative prompts, PromptBase is a hub for AI prompt enthusiasts2. Here are some featured prompts:

1

Video Game Logos With Text: Elevate your logo design skills.

1

Travel Vintage Postage Stamps: Explore stamps from around the globe.

1

Vintage Surreal: Discover sepia prints with a touch of surrealism.

90s Cinema Stills: Take a nostalgic trip through cinematic history.
